RNI Registration

It is essential for every newspaper, circular, magazine, or foreign publication in India to obtain an RNI registration from the Registrar of Newspapers for India. We can help you begin the process of registering for RNI so that you can start your own newspaper. Contact us to get the process started.

Step 1

It is very first and vital step for RNI Registration that the title of the Newspaper be verified by the RNI. This is to make sure that the title is accessible and to ensure a smooth registration process in the future. If you want to save your precious time, then consult our experts before applying title so that there is no hurdle in your title approval.

Before applying the title, it is also important to have a look at the guidelines of Title Verification.

Step 2

As soon as you have received the title verification letter from RNI, you need to present it to the authority concerned (DM/DC/SDM/DCP etc.), along with your declaration (Form-I) for authentication. If the publisher and printer are different, separate declarations should be submitted for each.
